*Sea Potato (Manatee)*


**Potato Weather** is a Cross-platform weather app for Android, Windows, Linux, and possibly more!

Written in Flutter

# Features:
**Current Temp/Weather**: *(fancy)* Self explanitory.

**Forecast**: Daily Forecast.

**Radar**: Radar supplied by the [National Weather Service](https://weather.gov "NWS Website") and [NOAA](https://noaa.gov "National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Website").

**Alerts**: Any EAS alerts for your area will be displayed.

**NOAA Weather Radio**: Out of Service currently. Please let me know if you know of any mp3 streams for the weather radio!!! 

**Snow Day Calculator**: Link to [snowdaycalculator.com](https://snowdaycalculator.com "Snow Day Calculator Website") *#notsponsored*
